Games

AREA 27

Area 27 is located somewhere in the Nevada deserts. It was created after a nuclear attack carried out by the Chaos Insurgency. All SCPs were transported from the old facility to the new one.

SKY WARS

Welcome to SKYWARS! Find yourself on a floating island where you can choose to mine ores, build your fortress, and battle your way until you are crowned victor of the sky!

AREA 47

Area-47 is an underground facility that contains anomalous objects that defy the laws of human nature. Play today as a scientist, security team member, or be a member of the anti-foundation Chaos Insurgency!

Omega Tower Defense Simulator

Welcome to Omega Tower Defense Simulator! Buy and collect all of the different towers, use your collected towers to defend yourself from various enemies, work together with your friends and prove yourself in the Hall of Heroes!

SCPF

The Special Containment Procedures Foundation is a government arranged organization with the widespread goal of containing anomalous objects that obstruct the normalcy of the real world, or so the people think. The foundation is run by a group of highly knowledgeable people that are said to never die in the darkness of the foundation. The foundation has the goal of Securing these anomalies, Containing these anomalies, and Protecting these anomalies.
